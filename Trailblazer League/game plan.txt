Game plan: Rush Kandarin
- Bank everything and bring small fishing net + axe + tinderbox + all food + 3 logs
- Do misthalin mystery quest for 16 crafting (keep knife)
- Fish shrimp until 8 fishing + 8 cooking (free net south of lumbridge)
- Get 18 agility at draynor course
- Talk to Morgan (2) to start vampire slayer
- Take garlic upstairs
- Grab 10k from stronghold
- Buy hammer and chisel from Varrock and bring it for cutting gems later
- Do varrock rooftops until reasonable agility lvl depending on tasks (maybe 40?)
- Do museum quiz for 20 hunter + 20 slayer
- Clean finds until 51 kudos and digsite pendant and 2 CUT opal/jade
- Talk to information clerk for 20 mining
- Tea stalls until 20-40 thieving (whatever ends up being good for tasks)
- Buy fire staff + 500 fire strike casts
- Buy a beer and talk to Dr Harlow in the pub (2) for vampire slayer. Talk again to receive a stake.
- 4t iron ore with knife + log southeast or southwest from varrock until 28 mining, bank all ore. Skip this if too crowded (and also skip mining gold in Karamja diary later) and get mining done later at fossil island.
- Go to edgeville fairy ring, fire strike tree spirits (BKQ fairy ring) until mith + addy + rune axe or out of runes
- Regular trees until lvl 3 wc + fm + fletching then oaks. Make highest lvl fletching item with all logs. Continue until 34 fletching. Keep and bank ~20 oak logs.
- Bring small fishing net, 2 CUT opal/jade + axes + pickaxe + fire strikes + cash + some food if available, fairy ring to CKR and complete karamja easy diary (do tasks south first then north)
- Sell gems for 100+ trading sticks, cut full inv of teak logs
- Go to brimhaven and grab karamja gloves 1 + lamp. Use lamp on ranged or runecrafting.
- Take cart to shilo village
- Buy fly fishing rod + 200(?) feathers from fising shop
- Buy pestle and mortar, spade, rope and 3 bronze bars from general store. If bronze bars are sold out, mine and superheat 2 bronze bars later when going back to varrock.
- Do 3t fishing + cooking until reasonable fishing + cooking lvls depending on tasks (40s?), bank all food

- Home teleport
- Optional: Buy Chronicle + some teles if you have money left
- Finish vampire slayer (hammer + stake + garlic + 10-15 or more fire strikes + food)

- 3t oaks + firemaking (decent spot at Varrock) until reasonable firemaking lvl (40?) depending on tasks
- Get lvl 3 smithing with the bronze bars

- 3t iron on fossil island near bank. Superheat the ores with nature runes from tree spirits. Continue until 10 nats left (save those) and 40(?) mining depending on tasks.
- Make iron knives with all iron bars (min 300, i.e. 60 bars), maybe some platebodies too
- Finish Karamja easy diary if left unfinished earlier due to gold mining step

- Train ranged on slayer tasks (or any monster in lumbridge) until 45 ranged

- Do varrock easy diary if still need tasks for Kandarin. To get into earth altar, catch young imps in puro puro until 24 hunter and lockpick, then earth imps until earth tiara.
